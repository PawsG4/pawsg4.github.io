FAQs

What is your purpose?

Our purpose is to help stray animals find their comfortable homes and owners that will show them love, affection and attention.

Are the pets vaccinated?

The reason for interview is to know if the animals is comfortable to live their and we also want to know if you're going to treat them right.

Is there a cost in adopting them?

No. We just want to rescue them and find someone that will love them and takes care of them.

Does the pets have any behavioral issues that might affect the decision of the adopter?

Yes. We will inform the adopters if the pet they wanted to adopt have any behavioral issues.

Is the pet healthy or has any sickness?

For the pets that has sickness they will be cured and they won't be available for adoption. We will not let them die because there will be no more reason for us to keep taking care of strays if we let one die.
